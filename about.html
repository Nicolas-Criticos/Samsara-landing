The Project

Bringing 16,000 trees back to life.

My name is Nicolas Criticos — electrical engineer turned farmer, stewarding VrischGewagt Boutique Olive Farm in the Swartberg Karoo. Thirteen years ago, 16,000 olive trees were planted here. Years of drought and neglect left less than 1% bearing fruit. I'm bringing them back — block by block, tree by tree.

Where we are

5,000 trees. Halfway there.

I'm currently rehabilitating the first 10 blocks — about 5,000 trees. Blocks 1–3 have full irrigation restored and the trees are responding. Blocks 4–6 have water back online but need soil and canopy work. Blocks 7–10 haven't started yet.

The work: repairing decades-old drip systems, rebuilding soil with compost and biochar, heavy structural pruning, and targeted nutrition timed to the seasonal cycle. Every bottle of Samsara oil directly funds this.
