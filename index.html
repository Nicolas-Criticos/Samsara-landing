The Story

16,000 trees.
One family.
One purpose.

Nestled in the ancient Swartberg Karoo — where the mountains meet the desert sky — we found 16,000 olive trees, long forgotten. Neglected for years, they still held life in their roots.

We made a vow: to bring them back, one by one, by hand. No shortcuts. No shortcuts. Only patience, soil, sun, and intention.

This oil is the fruit of that work. Every bottle carries the story of a tree returned to the earth.
