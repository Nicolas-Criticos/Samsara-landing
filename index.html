The Story

16,000 trees.
One family.
One purpose.

Deep in the Swartberg Karoo, 16,000 olive trees were planted thirteen years ago with a vision for abundance. Years of drought, neglect, and hardship left them struggling — less than 1% bearing fruit.

We are bringing them back. Block by block, tree by tree. Every bottle you buy directly funds this restoration.
